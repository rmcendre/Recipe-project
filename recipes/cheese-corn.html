Odin Cheese Corn

Cheese corn is a Koren comfort food made with corn, maynonnaise and cheese.

This cheese corn recipe is a base for how you would like to fix it. Add in some hot peppers, some ham or crab. You can just make it your own, and really can't go wrong. Personally I like my Chsse Corn with some crushed red peppers, and diced fried Spam.

Ingredients

  • Corn
  • Mayonnaise
  • Shredded Mozzarella
  • Sugar
  • Green Onions
  • Salt and Pepper

    • Tools needed

  • Mixing Bowl
  • Wooden Spoon
  • Small skillet or baking dish

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 400ËšF.
  2. Combine all the ingredients into a mixing bowl and stir together. Season with salt and pepper and give it one more good stir.
  3. Pour mixture into a lightly greased baking dish.
  4. Bake corn cheese for 10 minutes to melt the cheese and heat through.
  5. Transfer the dish into the broiler and broil for about 2 minutes to make it bubbly and brown on top.
